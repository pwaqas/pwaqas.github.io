Introduction

I am working in the team of Dr. Thorsten Wuest, as a postdoc researcher at the Department of Industrial and Management Systems Engineering, West Virginia University, USA.

I received my master's degree in computer science from the University of Agriculture Faisalabad, Pakistan, in 2017. Following that, I pursued a Ph.D. degree at the Machine Learning Laboratory in the Department of Computer Engineering, Jeju National University, Jeju, South Korea.

Previously, I served as an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing, Gachon University, South Korea, and worked as a Lecturer for the Department of Computer Science at the University of Agriculture Faisalabad. Additionally, I gained research experience as a Researcher at the Chongqing Key Laboratory of Cyberspace and Information Security, Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Chongqing, China.


My research interests include the  following areas

  • AI & Lachine Learning
  • Time Series Data
  • Blockchain 
  • Internet of Things.
  • Smart Manufacturing
